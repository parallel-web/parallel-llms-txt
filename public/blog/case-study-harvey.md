# How Harvey is expanding legal AI internationally with Parallel
================================================================

Harvey is the leading AI platform for legal and professional services. As they expand beyond common-law jurisdictions into civil-law countries across Latin America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific, they're partnering with Parallel to power web search across their platform and solve a fundamental data access problem: the specialized legal knowledge their AI needs often doesn't exist in any searchable index.

Tags:Case Study

Reading time: 3 min

!How Harvey is expanding legal AI internationally with Parallel

## Key highlights
-----------------

* - Harvey uses Parallel to power general web search across its AI platform
* - Harvey is expanding to new jurisdictions, partnering with Parallel to search over a specialized private index of hard-to-reach international legal domains
* - Both teams are working towards a goal of covering 60+ countries

## Powering Harvey's web search with Parallel
---------------------------------------------

Harvey chose Parallel's Search and Extract APIs to power general web search across their platform. Parallel's public web index offers the most comprehensive coverage of legal domains available. Where most search providers optimize for breadth alone, Parallel balances breadth with depth—investing in infrastructure to thoroughly crawl the highest-value content on the web. The result is that lawyers using Harvey get the most accurate and relevant data available from the public web.

## A specialized private index for the hardest-to-reach international legal sources
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

For the toughest domains— international legal sources like court rulings from Brazil's state tribunals, regulatory codes from Argentina, and committee resolutions from South Korea— Parallel has built a specialized private index that Harvey can access and directly search over.

These legal domains were never designed for AI to access. They feature dynamically rendered pages, deep PDF archives, and complex navigation that standard crawlers typically cannot handle. Many of these domains have never been indexed by Google or any foundation model training pipeline. The data exists, but it's effectively invisible to AI.

Building the infrastructure to reliably crawl these domains requires significant time and resource investment that would distract Harvey from their core product. Parallel's specialized index is built on proprietary crawl, retrieval, and ranking infrastructure purpose-built for exactly this problem - making previously inaccessible legal knowledge searchable at scale.

## Scaling specialized web search infrastructure to 60+ countries
-----------------------------------------------------------------

Harvey and Parallel are partnering to crawl and index thousands of legal domains across every market Harvey serves, giving Harvey's AI access to authoritative source-linked legal knowledge anywhere in the world.

> > *\_"One of the biggest bottlenecks to serving lawyers globally is access to authoritative legal data. Court rulings, regulatory codes, and legislation in dozens of countries are published on sites that no search engine has ever indexed. Parallel is solving that for us at a scale we couldn't build in-house."\_*

> >  — Gabe Pereyra, President & Co-Founder, Harvey

Every jurisdiction we index represents a new addressable market for Harvey and a new set of legal professionals who can trust AI to do real work. We're thrilled to be partnering with Harvey in this phase of their business.
